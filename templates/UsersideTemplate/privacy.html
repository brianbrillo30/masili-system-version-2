Data Privacy

Your data privacy rights are protected by the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012.

• The right to be informed.
If your personal data will be handled or already has been, you must be informed.
• The right to access.
You have the right to reasonable access, upon written request, to the nature and circumstances of the processing of your personal data.
• The right to rectify.
You have the right to request urgent rectification of any mistakes in your personal data if necessary.
• The right to erasure or blocking.
Based on reasonable basis, you have the power to prevent, remove, or request the disposal of your personal data.
• The right to file a complaint.
You have the right to file a complaint if you believe that your personal information has been unlawfully used, maliciously disclosed, inappropriately disposed of, or that any of your data privacy rights have been infringed.
• The right to damages.
If your rights and freedoms as a data subject have been violated by the use of personal data that was inaccurate, incomplete, out-of-date, false, illegally obtained, or unauthorized, you may be entitled to compensation.
• The right to data portability.
This right guarantees that YOU have complete control over YOUR data. Data portability enables you to securely retrieve, move, copy, or transfer your data electronically for later use.

We are collecting your data when you:
• Register
• Request a document

• Name, age, sex, civil status, date and place of birth, nationality and/or citizenship, address, and contact details
• Other information that you have directly provided us
