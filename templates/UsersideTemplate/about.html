ABOUT BARANGAY MASILI




About Barangay Masili

The Barangay is the smallest unit of the Philippine government. Naming the barangays when the first Philippine settlers arrived in the Philippines was made in different ways. Some were named according to their use like Brgy. Masili which comes from, “Chili garden”.

Barangay Masili is a barangay in Calamba City, Laguna. It has a population of 3827 residents.

It has 3 educational institutions:
-Pre-Elementary - Masili Day Care Center
-Pre- Elementary & Elementary - Tiyani Elementary School
-Secondary - Castor Alviar National Highschool

Barangay Chairman:
Eliseo G. Maligaya
